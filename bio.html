About Me

Early Life

I grew up in Georgia until the age of about 8, then I moved with my family to, first, Wyoming then after 6 months, to Washington state. There, my family stayed for about 4 years. After that, we moved back to Georgia, that way we could be closer to family.

High School/Post High School

I went all 4 years to Bleckley County High School, then because of COVID, I left to serve a mission in Utah for 3 months, before making it to my original assignment of Guatemala, where I spent the next 20 months.

Post Mission

After the mission, I went and sold Pest Control door to door in Orange County California I did that job from May to mid August. It was a lot harder than I thought it would be! I went home to my family in August, then in Early September, my Mom and I drove 29 hours non stop to Utah, where I currently am.
